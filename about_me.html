Favorite Restaurant

Dakzen in Davis Square has some of the best Thai Food I've had in the States.
I highly recommend their Khao Moo Dang or Khao Soi. All of their dishes are very authentic and delicious.

Favorite Food

My favorite dish would have to be a classic
Thai pork congee. This dish is usually served
with a poached egg, crispy fried dough, ginger,
and green onion
